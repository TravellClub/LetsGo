More Details Ruwanwelisaya

* The Ruwanwelisaya is a stupa, a hemispherical structure containing relics, in Sri Lanka, considered sacred to many Buddhists all over the world.

* It was built by King Dutugemunu.

* 140 B.C., who became lord of all Sri Lanka after a war in which the Chola King Ellalan, was defeated.
